In this section I will speak about LOL or “League of Legends” and its competitive mode.

LOL is a game that always plays in the same map, the game is formed of 2 teams with 5 players in each team. Each player chooses a character of the game. The aim of the game is to destroy the opponent nexus. In order to achieve it, the team will have to destroy the towers and the inhibitor of the opponent. The players will have to kill NPCs which will drop gold after being killed, and you will get experience, with that you will be able to buy objects and upper your level.

Players' roles

Top lane. This player plays in the top lane of the map. Until a year ago his principal objective was to absorb the damage of the opponents and stunned them. But now this role has changed a bit and the player could use magicians and assassins which cause a big harm to the opponents.

Top Lane

Top lane. This player plays in the top line of the map. Until a year his principal object was absorb the damage of the opponents and stunned themes. But now this role changed a bit and the player could use mages and assassins that cause a big damage in the opponents.

Mid Lane

Mid lane. This player plays in the middle of the map. His role is very important for the situation. As the player is in the centre of the map, he will have further information about the opponents and will share it with the other lanes in his team. He could move to the other lanes very fast and support them.

Bot Lane

Bot lane. The bot laners play in the bottom of the map. This lane is played for two players, the ADC and the Support. The ADC is the player who has the objective of kill the opponents. This character inflicts a huge damage, but he is very weak. The Support will have to protect the ADC and if it is necessary give his live to save the ADC. The support will have to put wards that give vision to the team. This character normally can stunt or lock the opponent while the team will have to kill them.

Jungle

This player plays in the jungle of the map. This role is a bit complicated, the player will have to follow an adequate pathing, in order to get in the appropriate time a level of experience necessary to help the other lanes. A Jungle with an inadequate pathing or who stays behind to the rivals’ jungle can occur in loss of the game for the team.

Currently the core of the game is the teamwork and the good communication of the team. An individual’s skills are important but in a high level, one player can´t win for himself.

Main regions of LOL

There are 4 principal regions in the competitive LOL. Them are LCK, LPL, LEC and LCS.

LCK

The League of Legends Champions Korea was dominating the game winning all World Championships except the first one (for non-participation) and the last one. This region stands out for the high discipline of the players, good player skills and unbelievable teamwork. The problem of the region in the last World Championship was that they could not adapt to a new macro game. In the past, the game used to develop in a mid or late game having games that lasted between 30-50 minutes. But in the last World Championship, the game was oriented to an early game, having games that lasted between 20-35 minutes. The early game was the weakness of Korean teams.

This region has legendary teams like SKT Telecom T1 winner of 3 World Championship, KT Rolster and the team of Samsung winner of 2 World Championship, that now is a disbanded team.

The LCK has legendary players, such as Faker who, at the age of 23 years old has won all competitions. Other fantastic players to follow this year are Chovi, Khan, Tarzan, Viper…

LPL

The Lol Pro League is the league of China. In this region there is the team that won the last World Championship, Invictus Gaming. The LPL is the biggest league with 16 teams. This region is very attractive, the teams are fighting all the time, and you can watch very rare characters. More that 95% of fans of the LOL are from this region.

The legendary teams of this region are Invictus Gaming, the current winner, and Royal Never Give Up.

Some of the best players are Rookie, TheShy, Doinb, Uzi…

LEC

The League of Legends European Championship is a region with a lot of talented players. The teams of this region improved a lot their macro game, and now it is one of the most powerful regions.

This region has legendary teams like Fnatic, winner of the first Worlds Championship and finalist of the last one. Other fantastic teams are G2, the dominator of last year with probably the best team of the history of the region. Another charismatic team is Origen, with one of the largest fan bases in Europe, despite having been absent of the competition for 2 years. They have returned and now they play at a very high level.

Some of the best players are Caps, Wunder, Perkz, Rekkles and Nukeduck.

LCS

The League Championship Series is the league of North America. This region is very particular. The region has some problems finding local talent, so they try to make up for this by signing European players and paying them more than double than in Europe. Despite the big investment in international events, they fail spectacularly.

The good teams in this region are Team Liquid, Team Solo Mid and Cloud 9.

Some of the best players are Doublelift, Bjergsen and Licorice.

Other Regions - SLO

The Superliga Orange is the most powerful European league after LEC. It has many good players and a very good quantity of viewers. In this league the teams playing are academy teams of the LEC like G2 Heretics, Mad Lions Splice, Origen BCN, and other teams like Vodafone Giants (former LEC team).
